Dama Shop - Website Planning Document

Site Name

Dama Shop

This name represents a store aimed especially at women, focused on fashion, style, and confidence. It is simple, elegant, easy to remember, and perfectly reflects the purpose of the brand.

Site Purpose

The site provides a professional platform where customers can browse and purchase women's clothing and accessories. It includes organized catalogs, detailed descriptions, size guides, contact information, promotions, and updates. Its goal is to offer a pleasant, clear, and reliable shopping experience.

Scenarios

  • What new products or collections are available?
  • How can I know which size to choose to ensure the clothing fits me well?
  • Are there active promotions or discounts?
  • Where can I see the shipping, exchange, and return policies?

Color Schema

Below are the selected colors and their usage:

Primary Color – #B47AE2

Used in main headers (H1, H2), primary buttons, and highlighted elements.

Secondary Color – #9C89B8

Used in subtitles, borders, icons, and hover states.

Background Light – #FAF7FF

Main background color of the site and general sections.

Soft Accent – #E8D7FF

Applied to cards, product containers, and promotional banners.

Dark Contrast – #4E4B6A

Color for main text, the footer, and secondary buttons.

Typography

  • Poppins – used for headers (H1, H2, H3), navigation, and buttons.
  • Roboto – used for body text, descriptions, and forms.
  • Playfair Display – used for special titles and featured banners.
